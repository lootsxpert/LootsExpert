Frequently Asked Questions

Simply copy the product URL from Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra, or other supported sites, paste it into our search bar at the top of the homepage, and click Search. Price Graph will load the price history chart instantly.

Price history is a historical record of the price changes of a product over time. It lets you inspect if the current sale price is a real discount or if the vendor raised it beforehand.

A price tracker is an automated search tool that monitors product prices and stores the values. Instead of checking manually, you configure trackers that watch items and recommend best buying times.

A price drop alert is an automated notification (sent via Email or Telegram) triggered when a tracked product falls below your desired target price threshold.

Price tracking helps you save up to 40% on purchases by revealing price drop trends, identifying artificial sale hikes, and sending alerts directly to Telegram.
