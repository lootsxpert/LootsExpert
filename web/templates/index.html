Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common queries about price graphs, alerts, and tracking

Simply copy the URL of the product page from Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra, or any other supported site, paste it in the search bar above, and click search. We will scrape and compile its price details and generate the interactive price graph instantly.

If you find a product you want but the price is too high, you can set a target drop threshold. Once the price drops to or below your target, our system registers it and sends you an email notification or a Telegram alert.

We provide two bots: the Price Tracker Bot (for active alerts) and the Price History Bot (for quick graph lookups). You can click on the "Telegram Bots" dropdown link in the header navigation menu to connect and start messaging them directly.
