Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Everything you need to know about tracking price drops and shopping smarter with Price Graph.

How does Price Graph track e-commerce prices?

We deploy high-fidelity automated crawlers that monitor supported product pages periodically, recording current pricing, MRP list values, ratings, and image changes to build a timeline graph.

Which stores are currently supported?

We support 10 major e-commerce platforms: Amazon India, Flipkart, Myntra, Ajio, Meesho, Shopsy, Croma, Reliance Digital, Tata Cliq, and Nykaa. Any unsupported links are analyzed using our generic metadata parser.

How do I set up price drop alerts?

Registered users can go to a product details card and set a custom Target Price alert. You can also connect to our Telegram Bots (Price Tracker Bot & Price History Bot) to receive price drops in Telegram chat.

Is using Price Graph free?

Yes, all features on the website, mobile application, and Telegram bots are completely free to use.

How do I reset my password if I forget it?

When registering, you configure a security recovery question and answer. To reset your password, you must verify your security answer on the Reset Password page. Without it, account recovery is not possible.
